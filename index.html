Our Story

Meet Your
Growers

In 2025, the last local nursery serving our market farms permanently lost its organic certification. That left farmers with large commercial operations that don't serve small accounts, DIY propagation that pulls labor off the fields, or giving up organic certification entirely. We started Midpoint because those aren't real choices.
